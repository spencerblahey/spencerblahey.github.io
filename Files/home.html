About Me

A mechanical engineer with a goal of contributing to the field of technology. I love working on unique problems, rapid design and prototyping, hands-on assembly, collaborating with others and have recently begun my journey towards full-stack development on the side.

My professional career began in an underground mining environment, where I completed internships as a technician, and later a maintenance engineer. Throughout my degree, I worked on a variety of fascinating design projects, accumulated >2500 hours of volunteer experience, and achieved academic excellence. Upon graduating, I was determined to challenge the stereotypical path to success, and took some time off to gain unique experience.

Now, I could not be more excited to continue my professional dreams. I am eager to work for a technology oriented company in a fast-paced role as a research engineer, design engineer, hardware engineer, or something of the like. On my resume, I have listed the skills I possess which I believe are tailored to such positions, and would encourage you to contact me if you would like to chat.
